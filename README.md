> A curated list of tools, projects, frameworks, communities, and resources for building a more sustainable world.

Sustainability focuses on balancing environmental, social, and economic needs to ensure a livable future. This list is intended for developers, designers, researchers, educators, entrepreneurs, and changemakers working toward sustainable solutions.

## Overview & Frameworks

- [United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)](https://sdgs.un.org/goals) – 17 global goals for a better, more sustainable future.
- [Project Drawdown](https://drawdown.org/) – Solutions-based framework for climate action.
- [The Natural Step](https://thenaturalstep.org/) – Science-based approach to sustainability and systems thinking.
- [Doughnut Economics](https://www.kateraworth.com/doughnut/) – Economic model balancing human needs with planetary boundaries.

## Climate Action

- [Climate Action Tracker](https://climateactiontracker.org/) – Independent scientific analysis tracking climate action and progress.
- [ClimateClock](https://climateclock.world/) – Visual countdown to critical global climate milestones.
- [Carbon Brief](https://www.carbonbrief.org/) – Journalism and analysis on climate science and policy.
- [Climate Trace](https://www.climatetrace.org/) – Tracks real-time greenhouse gas emissions with satellite and AI data.

## Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

- [SDG Tracker](https://sdg-tracker.org/) – Global data platform tracking progress toward the SDGs.
- [Our World in Data: SDGs](https://ourworldindata.org/sdgs) – Interactive data visualizations and analysis for each goal.
- [SDG API](https://unstats.un.org/SDGAPI/swagger/) – Official API for UN SDG statistical data.

## Energy & Renewables

- [Open Energy Dashboard](https://openenergydashboard.org/) – Open-source platform to visualize energy usage and efficiency.
- [IRENA](https://www.irena.org/) – International Renewable Energy Agency’s data, reports, and global maps.
- [NREL Open Data](https://data.nrel.gov/) – Open datasets from the U.S. National Renewable Energy Laboratory.

## Sustainable Tech & Design

- [Low Tech Magazine](https://www.lowtechmagazine.com/) – Renewable-powered magazine about low-tech solutions.
- [Solar Protocol](https://solarprotocol.net/) – Web platform hosted across solar-powered servers.
- [Open Sustainable Technology](https://opensustain.tech/) – Open-source projects addressing sustainability challenges.
- [Green Software Foundation](https://greensoftware.foundation/) – Tools and principles for sustainable software design.

## Carbon Tracking & Offsets

- [Awesome Carbon Accounting & Offsets](https://github.com/awesomelistsio/awesome-carbon-accounting-offsets) – Curated list of carbon tracking and offset tools.
- [CDR.fyi](https://www.cdr.fyi/) – Live database of carbon removal purchases.
- [Pachama](https://pachama.com/) – Technology platform for forest carbon credits and nature-based solutions.
- [Carbon Badge](https://carbon-badge.com/) – Free badge displaying your website's carbon footprint and environmental impact.

## Sustainable Business & Finance

- [B Lab](https://www.bcorporation.net/) – Certifies B Corps that balance profit and purpose.
- [UN Principles for Responsible Investment](https://www.unpri.org/) – Framework for sustainable investment.
- [GreenBiz](https://www.greenbiz.com/) – News and analysis at the intersection of business, technology, and sustainability.
- [Green Claims Scanner](https://greenclaims-scanner.com/) – Detect and flag greenwashing on websites to ensure compliance with EU Green Claims Directive.

## Sustainable Agriculture & Food Systems

- [FAO Agroecology Knowledge Hub](https://www.fao.org/agroecology/en/) – Resources from the Food and Agriculture Organization.
- [Regeneration International](https://regenerationinternational.org/) – Movement supporting regenerative agriculture.
- [Open Food Network](https://about.openfoodnetwork.org/) – Open-source platform for local food distribution.

## Circular Economy & Zero Waste

- [Ellen MacArthur Foundation](https://ellenmacarthurfoundation.org/) – Global leader on circular economy strategy and innovation.
- [ShareWaste](https://sharewaste.com/) – App that connects people with food scraps to composters.
- [Precious Plastic](https://preciousplastic.com/) – Open-source project for plastic recycling systems.

## Policy & Advocacy

- [C40 Cities](https://www.c40.org/) – Network of cities committed to addressing climate change.
- [ICLEI](https://iclei.org/) – Global network of local governments advancing sustainability.
- [Citizens’ Climate Lobby](https://citizensclimatelobby.org/) – Advocacy organization promoting carbon fee and dividend policies.

## Communities & Organizations

- [ClimateAction.tech](https://climateaction.tech/) – Tech professionals working on climate solutions.
- [Extinction Rebellion](https://rebellion.global/) – International movement using nonviolent direct action.
- [350.org](https://350.org/) – Grassroots climate action movement.

## Open Source Projects

- [Open Sustainable Technology](https://opensustain.tech/) – Directory of open-source projects solving sustainability problems.
- [EN-ROADS Climate Simulator](https://github.com/climateinteractive/en-roads) – Open climate scenario simulator.
- [Solar Assistant](https://github.com/Solar-Assistant) – Monitoring tools for solar energy systems.
